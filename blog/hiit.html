5 HIIT Moves to Boost Your Cardio

Published on Oct 1, 2025

HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training) is a powerful way to burn fat, build endurance, and boost cardiovascular health even with short sessions. Push your limits with short bursts of intense exercise and quick recovery.Burn fat faster and boost your metabolism in less time. A fast-paced workout that keeps your heart racing and energy high.

1. Jump Squats

Engage your quads, glutes, and core with explosive power. Do 3 sets of 20 seconds each.

2. Mountain Climbers

Fire up your abs and shoulders while increasing heart rate rapidly. Do 3 to 4 sets of 20 seconds each.

3. Plank

A full-body HIIT move that challenges both strength and cardio capacity. Do 3 sets of 60 seconds each. Strengthen your core and improve balance with this simple yet powerful move. Engage your abs, shoulders, and glutes for a total body challenge. Hold steady every second builds endurance and stability.

4. High Knees

Drive your knees up to waist level while pumping your arms for maximum intensity. Do 3 sets for 30seconds each.

5. HIIT Sprint

Sprint at maximum effort for 30 seconds, then walk or jog for 20 seconds. Repeat for 5 rounds.

Alternate between 30 seconds of work and 20 seconds of rest. Always warm up properly and cool down afterward.
